FREE MAP TOOL "SIGHT".<br />
This is just a pet project for my CV, only front-end features for free hosting on Github Pages.<br />
You may use it, but don't forget to say me "thanks" and do something good around you. Or give me a job ^-^<br />
<br />
Current project features:

<ul>
<li>World map (OSM) in three projection types: 3D, 2D, and perspective ("Columbus view");</li>
<li>Calculation of geographic coordinates under the mouse cursor;</li>
<li>Selection of coordinate system ("WGS-84", "СК-42 м", "СК-42 °", "ПЗ-90.11");</li>
<li>Applying markers to the map with their coordinates;</li>
<li>Linear measurements on the map;</li>
<li>Map clearing tools;</li>
<li>Camera control tools;</li>
<li>Theme control (light/dark, plus four color palettes);</li>
<li>Fullscreen view;</li>
<li>Supporting for mobile (touchpad) devices.</li>
</ul>
Also if I have time I plan to add: area measurements, searching, navigation, L10n (eng), loading and visualizing user's raster, vector, and 3D images.<br />
